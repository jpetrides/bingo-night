Join Us for a Fun Evening!

The PTO is thrilled to host Bingo Night on Friday February 6th from 6pm - 8pm. We are going to have a wonderful evening of fun. For those of you who are new to St Anthony, Bingo night is a marvel of modern efficiency that requires strict adherence to a highly complicated 4 step process:

  1. Show up at the cafeteria with the family - even if you are a little late
  2. Eat pizza, other delicious food, and snacks
  3. Play bingo
  4. Win prizes and hang with friends
